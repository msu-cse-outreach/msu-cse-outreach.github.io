Project Ideas

Our current project ideas for art-related programs.

Interactive Map

Code an interactive map of the world about different cultures' art with links to games, videos, and pictures related to that geographic area.

Graffiti Wall

Code a digital graffiti wall in which people can write or draw their artistic ideas as a community.

Art Suggestions

Interactive quiz where questions about current mood lead to music and art recommendations.

Art Sharing

Share art and music through coloring book drawings and music-making games.

Learning Art

Link to online resources and local studios that will teach art and music.
