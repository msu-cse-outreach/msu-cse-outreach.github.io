Mailing Lists

We have set up several mailing lists for parents and club members to communicate with each other and with the club staff. Some mailing lists accept messages from the public, others accept messages only from specific groups. All lists are read and archived by the club staff, but are not moderated. See the table below.

List Reaches Who can post? Use for
info@spartangwc.org SGWC advisors and lead facilitators Public General questions about SGWC
organizers@spartangwc.org SGWC advisors and lead facilitators Public Questions about club operations
parents@spartangwc.org Parents and guardians of Coders List members Receiving updates on club activities, arranging carpool logistics, sharing resources for nurturing your child's interests, arranging social events, etc.
