Olive Garden is a casual American-Italian restaurant. The appetizers are lasagna dip, breadsticks, spicy alfredo chicken, seafood stuffed mushrooms, fried mozzarella sticks, calamari, classic shrimp scampi fritta,spicy shrimp scampi fritta, loaded pasta chips, lasagna fritta, house salad, Chicken and Gnocchi,Pasta e Fagioli, Minestrone, Zuppa Toscana and spinach artichoke dip. Some of the mediterranean entries are herb grilled salmon, salmon piccata, chicken piccata, shrimp scampi, chicken margherita, and chicken giardino. The meat entries are chicken piccata, chicken alfredo, tour of Italy, Chicken Parmigiana, Chicken Giardino, Chicken Margherita, Chicken Carbonara, Chicken Scampi, Chicken and Shrimp Carbonara, Stuffed Chicken Marsala, Braised Beef Gorgonzola-Alfredo, Braised Beef Bolognese with Pappardelle, and Braised Beef Bolognese with Pappardelle. The seafood entries are Shrimp Carbonara, Shrimp Alfredo, Shrimp Scampi, Seafood Alfredo, Salmon Piccata, and Chicken and Shrimp Carbonara. The desserts are S'mores Layer Cake, Warm Apple Crostata, Zeppoli, Lemon Cream Cake, Seasonal Sicilian Cheesecake, Tiramisu, Chocolate Brownie Lasagna, Black Tie Mousse Cake, and Dolcini. Olive Garden most signature dishes to me are the unlimited dishes and the breadsticks. I would rate Olive Garden 4.5/5 stars. Some of the salads are house, buffalo chicken, Classic Ceaser, Michigan Summer, Southwest Chicken, Beef and Arugula, and Quinoa Salad. the salads come iwth house made dressing like buttermilk ranch, bleu cheese, honey mustard, chipotle ranch, 1000 island Miso Vinaigrette, Sweet Onion Vinaigrette, Roasted garlic Balsamic, and E.V. olive oil and red wine vinegar.
