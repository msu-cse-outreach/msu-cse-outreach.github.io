Exercise

Exercise is one of the biggest contributing factors of health. If people don't exercise, they can become unhealthy.
Some ways to exercise are:
•Go for a brisk walk or jog every day.
•Jump rope for 5 to 15 minutes.
•Do jumping jacks for 5 to 15 minutes.
•Go for bike rides.
•Swim laps at your local pool or fitness center.
•Follow a daily excersize plan or workout.
