  • Apple Sandwiches

    COPYRIGHTED IMAGE DELETED

    Prep time: 10 mins
    Total time: 10 mins
    Serves: 4

    Description:Apples slices topped with peanut butter, granola, and raisins make a delicious and healthy snack.
    Ingredients
    •2 Apples
    •2-3 tbsp Peanut Butter
    •3 tbsp Granola
    •A handful of Raisins
    •Apple Sandwiches

    Instructions
    1.DIRECTIONS
    2.Remove cores from apples and cut into ¼-1/2" thick slices
    3.Spread apple slice with peanut butter.
    4.Top with granola and raisins.
    5.Place another apple slice on top and gently press together.
    6.Repeat with remaining apple slices.
