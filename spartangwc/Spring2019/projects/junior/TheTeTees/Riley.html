Costumes!

Costumes are a key component of theatre and critical part of the success of a show/production.

Thank you Quizlet for the definitions and explainations:

"Elements of Costume Design

Texture

    The roughness or smoothness of the fabric.

  • [A.] ROUGH- (denim, burlap, velvet) -tend to absorb light -blend in to surroundings
  • [B.] SMOOTH- (silk, satin, linen) -tend to reflect light -stand out from surrounding
  • [C.] LAYERED- (fringe, sequins, appliqu√©) -attract attention

Pattern

    The design printed on or woven into the fabric.

  • [A.] SOLID- one color all over
  • [B.] WASH-dyed to mimic fading
  • [C.] SIMPLE PRINT- geometric shape repeated across the fabric
  • [D.] BUSY PRINT- irregular or inconsistent color/shapes
  • [E.] MESSAGE- words/images printed on the fabric

Colour

    The hue and value of the fabric.

  • [HUE]- where the frequency of a light wave falls on the electromagnetic spectrum. ( The name of the color)
  • [VALUE]- the brightness or darkness of a hue.
  • [VALUE CONT'D]- High-pale True-pure Low- dark/shadowy [Red]: Love, passion, anger, blood, violence [Orange]: optimism, energy, superficial, autumn, fire [Yellow]: cheerful, impatient, cowardly, sickly [Green]: balance, harmony, growth, life, money, greed, jealousy [Blue]: sadness, calm, peace, loneliness, loyalty, ocean [Purple]: creative, mysterious, royal, immature [Brown] earth, poverty, dirty, solid [Black]: death, power, scary, uncertain [White]: cleanliness, purity, innocence, snow

Fit

    How the clothes hang on the actor's body.

  • [A.] POOR- too baggy/ tight
  • [B.] RACK- fit well, but not specific to one person
  • [C.] TAILORED- clothes have been altered to suit one specific individual
  • [FIT]- is the biggest indicator of social status.
    • Poor-Low, Rack-Middle, Tailored-High

Silhouette

    The shape or style of the clothing.

  • [A.] HISTORICAL PERIOD- the clothes reflect the fashions of the time period of the play.
  • [B.] OCCUPATION- what the character does for a living.
  • [C.] TIME OF YEAR- reflects the season.
  • [D.] TIME OF DAY- reflect activities usually performed at a given time of day.
  • [1.] Work/School
  • [2.] Casual
  • [3.] Special Event
  • [4.] Night Clothes"
