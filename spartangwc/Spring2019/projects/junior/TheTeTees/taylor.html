Set and Props

Sets and Props have been used for years in plays and movies. Take your favorite movie and think about all the places the characters went and all the stuff they used. That was all created by set and prop.

What is the Diffrence Between Sets and Props

Sets

Sets are the background of the play/movie you will be in. Sets are things in the background to show where and when the charaters are. Sets aren't built into the stage and are moved on and off the stage after each scene. Some sets are even created by a green screen. Green screens make it so you can edit in backgrounds and special effects.

Have you ever seen Harry Potter? How about Star Wars? Everywhere those characters went, all those places. They were all something called 'sets'.

Props

Props are the things characters hold in their hands. Props are portable things that actors use to show what they are doing. (such as balls, pencils, stuffed animals, ect.)

In Harry Potter a prop would be a wand and in Sar Wars a light saber. These are both portable things that the characters use to show the action in the movie. Imagine how funny they would look with out these props.
