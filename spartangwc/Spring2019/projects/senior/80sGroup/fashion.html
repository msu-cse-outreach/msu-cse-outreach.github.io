Fashion in the 1980s were all about big, bold, bright and uterly glamorous.

Hair and Makeup

Matching the bold clothing the hair and makeup of the '80s were also intense. During this decade the hair was very big and wild. Full volume was the look everyone wanted. Wheather it was up or down it was full of hairspray. The makeup of the 80s were very dark and intense also. Bright blush and bold lipstick was very popular.
