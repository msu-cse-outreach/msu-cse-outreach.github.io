Wardell Stephen Curry II was born March, 14, 1988 in Akron Ohio. Being the son of of NBA veteran Dell Curry, he was surrounded by the game at a young age. He later attended Davidson (North Carolina) College and made his mark averaging 21.5 points per game as a freshman. He was then drafted in 2009 to the Golden State Warriors and is currently getting paid $34 million.
