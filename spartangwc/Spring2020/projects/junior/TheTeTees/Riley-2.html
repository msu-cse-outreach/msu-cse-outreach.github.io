Tips and Tricks!

Here are a few ways to improve your performance and tackle your stage fright!

Repetition

One helpful way to perfect your performance and to memorize your script is through repetion.

  • Make a constant practice time. For example: every day at 3 PM.
  • Go over your lines multiple times during alloted practice time.
  • Set alarms or reminders to remind you to practice.

Simulation

Another way to better your theatre perfomance and beat stage fright is simulation.

  • Bring together some of the other performers to practice together.
  • Practice in front of your family and/or friends to simulate the audience.
  • If possible, go to the theatre or wherever the perfromance will take place, and practice there.
