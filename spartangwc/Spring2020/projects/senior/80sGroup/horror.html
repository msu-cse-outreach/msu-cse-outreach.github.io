Background

Cujo is a psychological horror flim and novel by Stephen King, about a rabid dog. On Stephen King trip to a mechanic during "the spring of 1977" he had issues with his motorcycle which led him to visit a auto shop in Bridgton, Main. He said his motorcycle died when he arrived at the shop, and moments later a Saint Bernard came growling and eventually lunged for his hand. Although the mechanic stopped the dog by hitting it with a wrench, King was still startled by the situation. This incident inspired King to make Cujo.

Stephen King owned dysfunactional Ford Pinto, which is the same car model that was in the novel/film.
