Day5

We made it through our first week! We'd like to thank and congratulate our students for their energy and enthusiasm; our students' parents for their ongoing support and encouragement; our mentors for making each day exciting, engaging, and entertaining; and our team for their organization, coordination and commitment to making this a reality. Without you all, we wouldn't be here.

Computer Science is challenging and exciting, learning to code is so important to teach students how to communicate and think logically. Coding helps students understand the world around them better. Coding fosters creativity and allows students to apply their creative thinking skills. Joining the MSU x Izmir Sev Coding Club is a great way to learn these skills in a challenging, engaging, and positive environment. The challenges you overcome, the views you enjoy, and the friends you make are what it's all about.

Before we dive into a full recap of our first meeting, we've got a few announcements.

Announcements

    1. Looking Back

    Can you believe we've already completed our first three meeting? Time flies when you're having fun! So far, we've covered the basics of Python and For Loops! Check out our resources page for the slides from each meeting.

    2. Looking Ahead

    Remember that we meet every day from 16:30-18:00 TRT through the 10th of June!

    3. Forms

    Don't forget to fill out the Treasure Box and Project Brainstorm forms!

    4. Questions?

    As always, please reach out with any questions / concerns / ideas / feedback to turkeycc@egr.msu.edu.

Summer 2022

We are excited to launch the first USS22 MSU x İzmir SEV Coding Club on Monday, 30 May! Thanks to the generocity of our sponsors and supporters, we were able to approve 24 students to enroll this semester. Participants will be put into a zoom for remote club meetings.

To attend club meetings by Zoom, a participant must have:

  • Submitted a MSU x İzmir SEV Coding Club Confirmation form.
  • Returned a signed Parent Consent form (.pdf).
Please notify turkeycc@egr.msu.edu if you experience difficulty with either form.

An email will be sent to participants notifying them of the Zoom information. Please white-list turkeycc@egr.msu.edu so that your mailer does not label mail that we send you as spam. Send an email to turkeycc@egr.msu.edu if you have not received a notice with your cohort information by Wednesday morning.
